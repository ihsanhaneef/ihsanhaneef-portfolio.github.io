Insight'24

The INSIGHT'24 is a technical fest fest conducted by CCSIT, CU Campus. The insight is a prestigious fest that has been conducting for more than 20 years. It include various games and events like technova, enigma, seminars and workshops. It have various competitions like web designing, debugging, debate, presentations, lan gaming etc. Every season it creates new technocrats and new opportunities. The national IT fest has been riveted by many aspirants.
